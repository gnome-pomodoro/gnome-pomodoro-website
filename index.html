About

This GNOME app helps to manage time according to Pomodoro Technique. It intends to improve productivity and quality of work by reminding you to take short breaks.

Pomodoro Technique is based on two principles:
  • focusing on work for limited time, about half an hour
  • and clearing your mind during breaks

This workflow can improve focus, physical health and mental agility depending on how you spend your breaks and how strictly you follow the routine. Otherwise, the app can still be used as a regular break timer.

If you’re curious about the name — Pomodoro comes from a kitchen timer, which can be used to keep track of time.

You can read more about Pomodoro Technique here.

Features

Pomodoro Timer

  • Customizability
  • Postpone pomodoro until active
  • Break reminders
  • Hotkey

Desktop Integration

  • Indicator
  • Fullscreen notifications
  • Lock screen notifications
  • Hiding notifications during pomodoro
  • IM status control
